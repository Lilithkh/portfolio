About me

Hi there, I'm Lilit. Currently, at Ultralabs, working on a range of products from diverse industries, I am a product designer living in Yerevan, Armenia. My experience in complex projects helped me develop strong analytical and organizational skills. Product Design

How I Work

My Working Process

Make it simple, but significant.

  • Brainstorming and Conducting
    the User Research

    Brainstorming consists of three phases: 1.Definition of the problem.
    2.Idea generation.
    3.Selection of the most viable ideas among proposed.
    Research implies several aspects: market research to define the presence of competitors, the definition of trends, the assessment of the product’s prospective longevity, etc. As a rule, this task is solved by marketers and business analysts.

    01.

  • Sketching, Prototyping
    and Testing

    Sketches allow to somewhat narrow down this very direction when choosing the main concept, and only then work with composition, layouts, edits, refinement of the concept, etc., would follow. Demonstrating the functionality, ways of user interaction with the product and its basic appearance.The creation of the prototype allows to avoid many errors and corrections in the future.

    02.

  • Design system, UI part
    and Development

    A Design System is the single source of truth which groups all the elements that will allow the teams to design, realize and develop a product. It will evolve constantly with the product, the tools and the new technologies.To create a successful UI, we can use combination of psychology, brand, and industry niche knowledge.
