Lithium-ion Batteries

Category : Website UX/UI design

Project summary

BatNav is a digital expert that provides automated engineering and procurement services that help you buy the right Lithium-ion battery.

BatNav help companies get the right battery at the right price. They don't buy and sell batteries. BatNav provide the knowledge and guidance so customers can make informed choices and manage risks.
BatNav will connect Buyers and Sellers and provide trusted world-class expertise. BatNav is a digital expert that provides automated engineering and procurement services that help you buy the right Lithium-Ion battery.

Problem statement

The main requirement was to create a platform that would help businesses solve their energy storage problem. Thus the business needed a spot where the customers interested in purchasing energy storage resources could find and explore the options available.

The Process

  • The first step was conducting stakeholder interviews to understand the main points of the business in relation to the market. Further the identification of the target audience was completed followed by competitor research.

    01.

  • After having identified the main required aspects of the project, we created and discussed low fidelity sketches with the stakeholders and after finalizing the main features of the platform, I created the high-fidelity screens and the prototypes for testing. For the creation of the UI components and the design system, atomic design principles were implemented to allow for quick and agile development and expansion of features.

    02.

The solution

The research conducted led us to the idea of the creation of the following:

  • A tech platform that would enable seamless integration of engineering and procurement across project phases. From feasibility to design to procurement, the platform should allow to create and manage the engineering deliverables.
  • A website that would present the main products offered
